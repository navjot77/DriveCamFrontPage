Safety Matters !!!

ProDrive Cam is not only about recording the video while you drive, but also saving the incidents to cloud, getting GPS location of the vehicle real time and detection of traffic signs. This requires the installation of Nvidia Tk1 on the vehicle. Our aim is to protect the business with false implication when some accident occurs. And also to improve the behaviour of the driver by monitoring the real time data from vehicle.

Track the Vehicle.

Track your fleet by Sitting At Your Home Or Office. We provide exact location of the vehicle on the road.

Detection of traffic Signs

This product also helps the driver real time with detection of Traffic Signs and alerting driver.

Record any Incident.

The device installed on the Vehicle will save the video when some accident occurs. This video will be sent to cloud and admin will have access to see the video of the accident. This could help the business against any false implication and will process the claims faster.
